ABOUT US 

		
		   
Free donation program is a mission driven, employee-owned business, that has
 served the nonprofit community by helping them with online 
fundraising and donor engagement since 2021.
 We have supported over 150,000 causes, and have been
 the technology partner for hundreds of community-led Giving Days.

We are a small but mighty team, so we know what it’s like 
to work for a nonprofit organization with limited resources to
 achieve your mission.We’re here to help, 
with a powerful online fundraising suite, best in class customer
 support, and fundraising strategy resources to help you succeed.

We provide a portfolio of state-of-the-art 
online and mobile products including; Online Fundraising, Donor Management, Team 
and Event Fundraising, Custom Donation Pages
 and Widgets, Giving Day Platform and Personal Crowd funding.
 We are at the forefront of function, experience, and style, and are 
committed to building you the best and boldest technologies in online fundraising.
